What is a Health Facility ?

This is a health service delivery structure within a defined geographical area, that has one or more units/ departments operating within it e.g pharmacy unit, laboratory department etc. Each health facility is usually alocated health workers and there is an officer in charge in the facility who are charged with delivering health services to the general public. Within a health facility are Community Health Units which are smaller health service delivery structures compared to health facilities they are associated to.

In order to locate health facilities closest to you the MFL has plotted the location of these health facilities on a map to enable individuals locate the Health Facilities closest to them with the particular services offered.
