About the Master Facility List (MFL)



The Master Facility List is a Ministry of Health initiative to avail an online platform for the public to query and locate health facilities in Kenya. A user can search and/or filter by various facility attributes.

Discoverability through the MFL Geolocator Service
The Geolocator Service visualizes administrative units at national, county, constituency and ward levels, which shows facilities in that particular ward.
