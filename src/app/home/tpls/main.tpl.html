KENYA MASTER HEALTH FACILITY LIST
ABOUT THE KMHFL

Kenya Master Health Facility List (KMHFL) is an application with all health facilities and community units in Kenya. Each health facility and community unit is identified with unique code and their details describing the geographical location, administrative location, ownership, type and the services offered.

OUR FACILITIES

There are two types Health Facilties and Community Health Units explained below in terms if KMHFL.

WHAT'S A HEALTH FACILITY?

This is a defined health service delivery structure that provides services and has one or more departments operating within it e.g. Outpatient, pharmacy, laboratory . In KMHFL, a facility is described by it’s unique code, ownership, type, administrative and geographical location, and services provided.

WHAT'S A COMMUNITY HEALTH UNIT?

This is a health service delivery structure within a defined geographical area covering a population of approximately 5,000 people. Each unit is assigned 2 Community Health Extension Workers (CHEWs) and community health volunteers who offer promotive, preventative and basic curative health services.

These are governed by a Community Health Committee (CHC) and each Community Health unit is linked to a specific Health facility.
