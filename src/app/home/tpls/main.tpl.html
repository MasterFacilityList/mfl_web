About the Kenya Master Facility and Community Units Database
Geolocate Facilities
The Facility Geolocator visualizes administrative units at national, county, constituency and ward levels, which shows facilities in that particular ward. Individual Community Health Units can be viewed on a map on the view showing their particular details.

The Kenya Master Facility and Community Units Database is a Ministry of Health initiative to avail an online platform for the public to query and locate health facilities and their corresponding community health units in Kenya. A user can:

Search for:

Health Facilities or Community Health Units.

Major search attributes include:

Facility or Community Health Unit Name or Code.

Other search attributes include:

Facility Type, Health Services, Number of Beds & Cots, Administrative Units (counties, sub-counties and wards) e.t.c.

What is a Facility?

This is a health service delivery structure within a defined geographical area, that has one or more units/ departments operating within it e.g pharmacy unit, laboratory department etc. Each health facility is usually allocated health workers and there is an officer in charge in the facility who are charged with delivering health services to the general public. Within a health facility are Community Health Units which are smaller health service delivery structures compared to health facilities they are associated to.

These are governed by a Community Health Committee (CHC) and each Community Health unit is linked to a specific Health facility.
What is a Community Health Unit ?

This is a health service delivery structure within a defined geographical area covering a population of approximately 5,000 people. Each unit is assigned 2 Community Health Extension Workers (CHEWs) and community health volunteers who offer promotive, preventative and basic curative health services.

A user can do the following with regards to Community Health Units:

  • Search for a Community Health Unit
  • View Details of a Community Health Unit
  • Rate a Community Health Unit
  • View a Community Health Unit on map
